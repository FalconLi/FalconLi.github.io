Bio

I am a year 4 undergraduate student at the University of Hong Kong (HKU), pursuing a major in Computer Engineering in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE). It is my pleasure to work with the JC STEM Lab of Intelligent Cybersecurity at HKU, supervised by Prof. Ho Chen. I also had the opportunity to collaborate with researchers at the University of British Columbia (UBC) during my Mitacs Globalink Research Internship in the summer of 2025, where I worked with the Systopia Lab under the supervision of Prof. Aastha Mehta.

My research interests lie in Computer Security, with a particular focus on AI-driven security. I am working on automated program analysis and vulnerability detection, applying AI-driven fuzzing to enhance software security testing. I am also interested in the field of Trustworthy AI and I am currently exploring topics in that area.
